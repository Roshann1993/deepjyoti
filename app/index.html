About us

The BMSBS community is a small, caring, cohesive, residential community firmly committed to the "common good." It was established in 2054 B.S.This School enjoys a reputation as one of the finest college-preparatory schools, and certainly one of the very top small boarding schools, in the country. The challenging academic program, prepares students for academic success and the rigors of college while cultivating in them a love of learning that becomes a lifelong habit.
